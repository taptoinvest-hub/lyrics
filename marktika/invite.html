Frequently Asked Questions

How does the invite program work?
Share your unique invite link with friends. When they sign up using your link, both you and your friend earn rewards. You can track your progress and rewards in real-time.
What rewards can I earn?
Rewards include premium feature access, extended free trials, exclusive discounts, early access to new tools, and special recognition in our community.
Is there a limit to how many friends I can invite?
No limits! You can invite as many friends as you want. The more friends you invite, the more rewards you earn. Top inviters get special ambassador status.
How do I track my invites and rewards?
Your invite dashboard shows real-time statistics including invites sent, successful signups, pending rewards, and your progress toward next reward tiers.
